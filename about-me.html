Timeline

This timeline highlights my journey from education to professional development, showcasing key milestones and experiences that have shaped my skills and career path.

From my early education in mathematics to my current role as a software developer, each step has contributed to my growth and expertise in the field. The timeline illustrates my progression through various roles, including internships, academic projects, and professional positions, emphasizing my commitment to continuous learning and development.

As I continue to evolve in my career, I look forward to new challenges and opportunities that will further enhance my skills and contribute to the field of software development.

2021 – 2023

Chingford Sixth Form (A-Levels)

Studied Computer Science, Maths, and Further Maths, achieving grades A, C, and D. Balanced academic studies, part-time work, volunteering, and hobbies.

2021 – 2023

A1 Events and Cleaners (Event Operations Team)

Worked in waste management and logistics at large-scale events. Managed radio communications, responded to issues rapidly, and collaborated with team members to efficiently allocate resources under strict time pressures.

2023 – 2025

University of Exeter (Mathematics BSc)

Completed two years of a Mathematics degree. Developed strong skills in Python, R, MATLAB, and LEAN. Achieved Firsts in solo and group projects, producing technical reports with LaTeX. Made a considered decision to leave before the final year to pursue a direct career path in software development.

2024

The Stables at Cowley (Front of House)

Worked in a high-pressure, customer-facing role dealing with large customer volumes. Contributed to a positive client experience while maintaining professional service standards in a team-based environment.

2025

Software Development Portfolio Launch

Designed and launched this portfolio website to showcase projects, share skills, and build a professional presence within the software development community. Actively pursuing new projects and opportunities to expand technical knowledge and experience.
